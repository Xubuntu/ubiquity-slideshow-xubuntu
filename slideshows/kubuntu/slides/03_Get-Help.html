Getting help with Kubuntu

  • If you need help, try Help from the menu, or the Help menu in most applications. Let us know about your Kubuntu experience at Kubuntu Community*!

  • In addition to our extensive written help, the Kubuntu community in conjunction with the Ubuntu community, provides free technical support in person and over the Internet. Learn more at Kubuntu Support**.

    • * http://www.kubuntu.org/community
    ** http://www.kubuntu.org/support
