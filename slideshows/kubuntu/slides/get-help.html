Getting help with Kubuntu

  • If you need help, try Help from the menu, or the Help menu in most applications.
  • In addition to our extensive written help, the Kubuntu community in conjunction with the Ubuntu community, provides free technical support in person and over the Internet. There is also commercial support available through Canonical, its partners and approved companies. Learn more at ubuntu.com/support.
  • Let us know about your Kubuntu experience at ubuntu.com/community!
