Getting help with Xubuntu

  • If you need help, try Help in the Applications menu, or the help menu in most applications.
  • There are more ways to get help, including:
  • Our IRC channel #xubuntu at the Freenode IRC network (Click here to join now!)
  • The Xubuntu-users mailing list
  • For a complete list of support methods, see the Help & Support -page in our website.
