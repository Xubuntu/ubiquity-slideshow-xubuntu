The Xubuntu desktop

On the desktop, you will see icons that can be used to access some of the most common places on your system. In addition, you will see a single, multipurpose panel at the top of the screen, much like this:

On the left hand side, you will see a Xubuntu logo. When you click it, the main menu is opened. We will tell you more about this menu in the next slide.

The middle part of the panel is dedicated to window buttons for open applications.

On the far right, you will see a clock and a date. Click the date to see the monthly calendar.

Left of the clock you will see the indicators, which give you information on your system, including network connectivity, battery (when appropriate), volume level, instant messages and more.
