Enjoy your music and videos

Listen to your favorite music with Audacious, a simple but powerful audio player. And enjoy all your videos with Gnome MPV, the next generation video player, with support for many formats.

You can also obtain additional codec support by installing the lubuntu-restricted-extras package.

Included software

  • Audacious

  • Gnome MPV
