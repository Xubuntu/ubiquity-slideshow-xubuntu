Grab some popcorn!

Ubuntu MATE comes with Firefox pre-installed and Google Chrome is in the Software Boutique so you can watch content from your favourite streaming services such as Netflix and YouTube.


With VLC installed by default you can watch all you local video with ease and Emby media server is one-click away from the Software Boutique to create your own streaming server.
