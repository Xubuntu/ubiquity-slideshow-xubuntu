Get up and running faster

Ubuntu MATE Welcome greets and assists new and returning users. The Getting Started wizard will guide you through the first boot post-installation setup and configuration of additional peripherals and devices and the documentation will help orientate new users. The Systems Specifications section will help you explore your hardware and offers one-click install and launch of many useful system monitoring tools. When you need support Ubuntu MATE Welcome is the jumping off point to the community.
