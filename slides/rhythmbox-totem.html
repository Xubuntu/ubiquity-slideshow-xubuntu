Music and movies in Ubuntu

  • Ubuntu is ready to play videos and music from the web and from DVDs. It works straight away for free and popular formats; the ones we can always use without paying license fees. In other cases, Ubuntu will ask for permission and then download the right software from the Internet.
  • Rhythmbox Music Player lets you organize your music and listen to Internet radio. It also keeps track of podcasts, to download new episodes of a show automatically.
  • Just plug in an MP3 player to start synchronizing your music collection, or insert a CD to copy its music to your computer.
