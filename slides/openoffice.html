Office tools at your fingertips

  • OpenOffice.org is a powerful office application that is very easy to learn.
  • It is useful to create letters, presentations and spreadsheets, as well as diagrams and databases.
  • Compatibility will be the least of your concerns, as OpenOffice.org can open and save documents for all the popular office applications, including WordPerfect and Microsoft Office. It uses the ISO standard OpenDocument format by default.
