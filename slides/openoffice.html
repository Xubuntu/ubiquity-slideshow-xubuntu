OpenOffice - Office tools at your fingertips

OpenOffice.org provides all of the functionality you expect from a powerful office application and in an easy to use format that you will get to grips with very quickly.

Whether you need to type a letter, create a presentation, calculate using a spreadsheet, store data in a database or even create mathematical formulae OpenOffice.org will have you covered, always keeping compatible with the formats that your friends, colleagues and clients are using.
