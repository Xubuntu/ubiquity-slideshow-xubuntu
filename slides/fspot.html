Make photos that last with F-Spot

  • With F-Spot Photo Manager, it is really easy to store photos on your computer. Just attach tags to your photos to describe them. Later on, those same tags help to quickly find a particular photo.
  • Double click a photo to do some quick touch-ups, for example to remove red-eye, crop a photo or change the brightness.
  • Finally, you can select some photos and use the Export option to write them to CDs, email them to friends or share them online with services like Picasa and Flickr.
  • To get started, open F-Spot from the Graphics Applications menu or attach a digital camera and follow the prompt that appears.
