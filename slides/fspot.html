Organize, enjoy, and share your photos

  • With F-Spot Photo Manager, it is really easy to share, touch-up and organize your digital photos.
  • Just attach tags to your photos to describe them. Later on, you can use those same tags to quickly find a particular photo.
  • Use the Export option to write your photos to CDs, email them to friends or share them online.
  • To get started, open F-Spot from the Graphics Applications menu or attach a digital camera and follow the prompts.
