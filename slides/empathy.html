Keep in contact with Empathy

  • With Empathy IM, Ubuntu can keep you connected right from the start. It works with all the major instant messaging networks, including Google Talk, Windows Live and Jabber.
  • You can change your status at any time by clicking your name at the top right of the screen.
  • Empathy hides in the Message Indicator, which looks like an envelope beside the clock. Any new messages get placed there, too, so you can respond to them when you want.
