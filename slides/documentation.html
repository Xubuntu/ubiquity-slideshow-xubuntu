Getting Help with Ubuntu

  • Need help? Just click on the button at the top of your screen to access the Ubuntu Help Center.
  • Can't find the answer? The Ubuntu community provides extensive free technical support. There is also commercial support available through Canonical, its partners and approved companies. Learn more at ubuntu.com/support.
  • Want to contribute? Ubuntu has a friendly community of contributors. You can get involved at ubuntu.com/community.

The installation will finish soon. We hope you enjoy Ubuntu.
