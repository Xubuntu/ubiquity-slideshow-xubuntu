Installing additional software

  • Take a look at the Software Center to install (and uninstall) software from our online repository, which we carefully organize to be safe and up to date.
  • There is great software for everything from making music and movies to creating 3D models and exploring the galaxy.
  • Software Center can also keep track of software from other places. Just check if they provide a repository designed for Ubuntu, or a Debian package, and install that.
